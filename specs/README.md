# Specifications

The specification area separates active design work from historical records.

## Active Work

- `future-ideas.md` contains unresolved ideas that do not yet have a focused
  RFC.
- `changes/` is reserved for active RFCs and their implementation notes. There
  are currently no active RFCs.

## Archive

`archive/` preserves implemented and superseded RFCs, completed implementation
records, and future ideas whose requested cleanup or design decision is done.
Archived material is retained as historical context and may describe
intermediate behavior that a later RFC superseded.

When an RFC is completed or superseded, move it into the appropriate archive
section, update its status, and add it to `archive/README.md`. Do not delete the
design record.
